Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Your data is stored securely and is accessible only by authorized personnel. We use industry-standard protection measures to prevent unauthorized access.

We do not sell your data. We may share it with third-party lenders or partners solely for the purpose of processing your application.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time. Please contact us to make such a request.
